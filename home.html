Top 4 Hotels to Visit in Jamaica

  1. S Hotel Jamaica               Ratings: 4.7/5

    Situated right at the center of the mesmerizing isle's tourism capital, S Hotel Jamaica is a Boutique All-Inclusive hotel that lends itself beautifully to an authentic Caribbean experience. Treat yourself to an enjoyable time with an abundance of tourist attractions positioned within close proximity to our luxury hotel in Montego Bay, Jamaica.


  2. Jewel Grande Montego Bay               Ratings: 4.5/5

    Escape to Jewel Grande Montego Bay Resort & Spa. Experience unparalleled luxury, sweeping Caribbean Sea views, premium amenities and the pampering you deserve. From cleansing treatments at our world renowned spa to limitless libations, delectable restaurants, and a variety of resort activities that will keep you entertained.


  3. Half Moon Montego Bay               Ratings: 4.5/5

    As a discerning traveller, you will enjoy the luxury of space and tranquility we have to offer. Imagine your first morning taking in the panoramic view of the horizon, and watching the sunrise having just concluded yoga meditation in our overwater bungalow. Then, cycle to the Seagrape Terrace for breakfast before beginning a day of whatever you want. Whether you choose to lounge on the beach, play a round of tennis or golf, visit a local art gallery, or go horse riding along the beach. Your days at Half Moon are yours to spend in any way you wish.


  4. Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall               Ratings: 4.5/5

    The all-inclusive Hyatt Ziva Rose Hall in Montego Bay, Jamaica, offers recreation and relaxation for the entire family. Nestled along the shores of the sparkling Caribbean Sea, blue skies and the scenic mountains of Jamaica serve as the perfect luxury vacation backdrop.
